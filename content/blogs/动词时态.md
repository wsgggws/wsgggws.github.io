## 核心理念

1.  **Be 动词也是动词**: 只要把 be 动词看作普通动词，句子就只剩两种状态：**简单式**与**完成式**。
2.  **简单式 (Simple)**: 用括弧型 `( )` 的时间段来表达（过去、现在、未来）。
3.  **完成式 (Perfect)**: 用箭头型 `->` 的时间流来表达（截止到某时）。
4.  **分词角色**: BE 动词后面的分词（V-ing / V-ed）其实是**形容词补语**。
    *   V-ing: 正在进行（主动/状态）。
    *   V-ed: 已经完成（被动/状态）。

## 1. 简单式 (Simple Tenses)

### 过去时间 (Past)
*   **Form**: Ved / was / were
*   **Concept**: 过去发生的事，现在已经结束。
*   **Timeline**: `.....( in 1979 ).....|now`
*   **Example**:
    *   The U.S **established** diplomatic relations with P.R.C. in 1979. (建立外交关系)
    *   I **was** a student. (Not anymore)

### 现在时间 (Present)
*   **Form**: V / Vs / am / is / are
*   **Concept**: 事实、真理、习惯。包含过去、现在和未来。
*   **Timeline**: `( --- fact --- )` includes `now`
*   **Example**:
    *   The sun **rises** in the east. (真理)
    *   I **play** tennis every weekend. (习惯)

### 未来时间 (Future)
*   **Form**: will + V
*   **Concept**: 将要发生。
*   **Example**:
    *   I **will call** you tomorrow.

## 2. 完成式 (Perfect Tenses)
*   **Form**: have/has/had + Vpp (past participle)
*   **Concept**: "Before and up to..." (在...之前，直到...)

### 现在完成式 (Present Perfect)
*   **Form**: have/has + Vpp
*   **Concept**: 截止到现在为止的经验、结果或持续动作。
*   **Timeline**: `---------->|now`
*   **Example**:
    *   I **have been** to Japan twice. (截止现在的经验)
    *   She **has lived** here for 10 years. (持续到现在)

### 过去完成式 (Past Perfect)
*   **Form**: had + Vpp
*   **Concept**: "过去的过去"。截止到过去某个时间点。
*   **Timeline**: `---->|past .....|now`
*   **Example**:
    *   When I arrived at the station, the train **had left**. (火车开走在先，我到达在后)

---

## 3. 进行时的本质
所谓的“进行时” (Simple Continuous) 其实是 `Be动词` + `现在分词(adj)`。
*   I **am** reading. = I am (in the state of) reading.
*   I **was** reading.

## 4. 被动语态的本质
所谓的“被动语态” 其实是 `Be动词` + `过去分词(adj)`。
*   The window **is** broken. = The window is (in the state of being) broken.
*   The house **was** built in 1990.

- Bush is the U.S. President.

```
    -------------(-------x--------)--------->
                        now
```

```
    -----------(-------x-------)---------->
```

### 未来时间(will+V, will+be+Ving, will+be+Ved)

- There will be a major election in March

```
    ---------x-----------(----------)--------->
            now             in March
```

- Don't call me at six tomorrow. I'll still be sleeping then

```
    --------x-----------(-----)-------------->
           now            then: at six tomorrow
```

- the building will be razed next month.

```
    --------x---------(---------------)--------->
           now          next month
```

## 完成时间

### 现在时间(has/have+Ved, has/have+been+Ving, has/have+been+Ved)

- I'm sure I have seen this face somewhere

```
         have seen  ------------
                                |
    ----------------------------x----------->
                               now
```

- We have been working overtime for a wwek to fill your order.

```
         have been  ------------
                                |
    ----------------------------x----------->
        for a week             now
```

- The house has been redecorated twice since they moved in.

```
        --- has been------------
       |                        |
    ---x------------------------x----------->
      moved in                 now
    语气重点是“算到现在是多久”
```

### 过去时间(had+Ved, had+been+Ving, had+been+Ved)

- Many soldiers had died from pnenumonia before the discovery of penicillin

```
         had died  ------------

    ----------------------------x-------------------x------>
                             discovery             now
```

- I had been smoking three packs of cigrettes a day before I decided to quit.


- Japan had not been defeated yet by the time Germany surrendered unconditionally
